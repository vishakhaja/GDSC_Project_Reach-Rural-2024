About Us

Our mission

Nurture each child’s passion, curiosity, optimism, and educational success.

Together, we can help the world rise. Most problems we deal with are too complex to be solved by one person. It takes passion to accelerate progress. It takes commitment to create change. But, it takes our collective efforts to transform lives and communities.

TRANSFORMING TOGETHER

Rural Reach was founded on August 20, 2019 by a group of friends, who believed that every underprivileged child deserves quality education. Since then, Rural Reach has transformed this conviction into a volunteering opportunity for India’s youth, launching a snowball effect of nurturing talent on the path to an educated, poverty-free India.

VISION

To help build a more influential, equal and socially conscious society.

MISSION

To drive social change by fostering an environment where young adults and children learn, lead, and thrive
